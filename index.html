Hi, here is how to play this GAME üòç

* you collect points by rolling the dice when it is your turn
* if you get any number other than failing numbers you will add that to your current score
* if you hold your current score it will be added to your scores store, which represent your real score
* if you get a failing number before holding your current score, you lose them
* the player wins when reaching a score of 100 in the scores store

difficulty explanation

Easy: one failing number, which is number 1, you have 17% of losing your current scores each time you roll the dice
Intermediate: two failing number, which is number 1 and 4, you have 33% of losing your current scores each time you roll the dice
Difficult: three failing number, which is number 1 and 3 and 5, you have 50% of losing your current scores each time you roll the dice
